Bricks Scan: Brand Identity System & Brand Book

1. BRAND STRATEGY

Brand Story

The Challenge: Real estate moves fast, but the paperwork moves slow. Agents, brokers, and property managers are bogged down by scattered physical documents, disorganized digital files, and clunky legacy scanner apps that feel like afterthoughts. The speed of closing a deal is often bottle-necked by the friction of capturing and retrieving information.

The Transformation: Enter Bricks Scan. Built not just as a scanner, but as a "workflow-first" toolkit tailored for the real estate professional. It transforms the chaotic stack of contracts, receipts, and property specs into cleanly structured, searchable, and calculate-ready files instantly on-device.

The Resolution: With Bricks Scan, real estate professionals regain their time and confidence. Information becomes clean, readable, and instantly understandable. Workflows shift from frustrating to effortless, empowering users to make clear decisions and close deals faster, all from a premium, privacy-first iOS experience.

Brand Personality

Archetype: The Sage meets The Magician. - The Sage: Represents clarity, wisdom, and truth. Bricks Scan cuts through the noise of paperwork to deliver clear, structured data. - The Magician: Represents transformation and effortless speed. Turning a crumpled piece of paper into a structured, searchable digital asset feels like magic.

Human Traits: - Meticulous but Unfussy: Cares deeply about the details (typography, spacing) but presents a simple interface. - Reliable/Professional: Predictable behavior, privacy-first, trustworthy. - Sharp/Crisp: Fast, modern, and highly responsive.

Voice and Tone Matrix

Dimension Stance Rationale & Application
Funny vs. Serious Focused & Serious (with a touch of wit) We represent a professional toolkit handling sensitive real estate data. We don't make jokes, but our copy is clever and sharp.
Casual vs. Formal Elevated Casual (Premium) Approachable but expert. Like a high-end concierge or a well-tailored suit. We avoid stiff corporate jargon but maintain respect.
Irreverent vs. Respectful Deeply Respectful We respect the user's time, privacy, and expertise. Our language is supportive and empowering.
Enthusiastic vs. Matter-of-fact Matter-of-fact We let the speed and magic of the product speak for itself. We use confident, active verbs rather than exclamation points.

Messaging Hierarchy

Tagline: Structure the chaos. Close the deal.

Value Proposition: Bricks Scan is the premium, on-device toolkit that turns your real estate paperwork into structured, searchable decisions—instantly.

Key Messages: 1. Workflow-First, Not Just a Scanner: Designed specifically for the fast-paced real estate environment (capture, structure, retrieve, calculate). 2. Effortless Speed: Everything happens instantly on-device. No loading spinners, no waiting for cloud processing. 3. Uncompromising Craft: A premium aesthetic that feels at home on your iOS device. Clean typography, thoughtful spacing, intuitive micro-interactions. 4. Absolute Control & Privacy: Your data stays yours. Predictable reliability with powerful options when you need them, simple defaults when you don't.

Proof Points: - On-Device OCR: Instant text extraction and searchability without internet dependency. - Native iOS Design: Fluid animations, haptic feedback, and a UI that prioritizes content. - Folder & Tag Systems: Tailored for property management and deal-flow organization.

2. VISUAL IDENTITY SYSTEM

Logo Concepts & Rationale

Direction 1: The Blueprint Wordmark (Chosen Direction) Rationale: A customized, geometric sans-serif wordmark where the "k" and "s" in Bricks lock together like masonry, symbolizing structure and stability. The "Scan" portion is set in a lighter weight to represent speed and digital transformation. It feels architectural yet highly technical.

Direction 2: The Viewfinder Symbol Rationale: An abstract icon combining the corners of a document scanner viewfinder with the shape of a house or building. It’s literal but elevated—instantly communicating "scanning" and "real estate."

Direction 3: The Stack Combination Rationale: A minimalist stack of three horizontal rectangles (representing documents or bricks) that transition in opacity from 100% to 20%, paired with a clean wordmark. It illustrates the concept of turning physical clutter into clean, organized digital data.

Logo Variations

  • Primary: Full color (Blueprint Wordmark in Charcoal, "Scan" in System Mint).
  • Secondary: Simplified (Just the 'B' monogram with viewfinder framing).
  • Monochrome: Solid Black.
  • Reversed: Solid White for dark backgrounds.
  • Minimum Size: 1 inch (25mm) wide for print, 120px wide for digital.
  • Clear Space: The height of the "B" in Bricks must be maintained on all sides as clear space.

Logo Usage Rules

Correct Applications: 1. Use the Primary logo on white or light gray backgrounds. 2. Use the Reversed logo on dark or photographic backgrounds with a heavy overlay. 3. Ensure the clear space rule is always respected. 4. Scale the logo proportionally. 5. Use the secondary monogram for app icons, favicons, and social avatars.

Incorrect Applications (DO NOT): 1. Do not stretch, squash, or distort the logo proportions. 2. Do not change the logo colors outside the approved palette. 3. Do not place the full-color logo on a busy background without sufficient contrast. 4. Do not add drop shadows, bevels, or physical effects to the logo. 5. Do not rotate the logo.

Color Palette

  • Primary Palette:
  • Charcoal (Primary Text & Logo): Hex #1C1C1E | RGB 28, 28, 30 | CMYK 70, 60, 60, 80. Psychology: Professionalism, authority, contrast.
  • System Mint (App Accent / Action): Hex #00C7BE | RGB 0, 199, 190 | CMYK 70, 0, 30, 0. Psychology: Energy, technical precision, speed, aligned with native iOS.

  • Paper White (Backgrounds): Hex #FFFFFF | RGB 255, 255, 255 | CMYK 0, 0, 0, 0. Psychology: Cleanliness, clarity, blank canvas.

  • Secondary Palette:

  • Real Estate Navy: Hex #0A2540 | RGB 10, 37, 64. Psychology: Trust, financial security.
  • Blueprint Blue: Hex #0050E6. Psychology: Planning, structure.

  • Status Red (Destructive): Hex #FF3B30. Psychology: Alert, deletion.

  • Neutral Colors (UI Grays):

  • Gray 100 (Cards): Hex #F2F2F7
  • Gray 300 (Borders): Hex #C7C7CC
  • Gray 500 (Secondary Text): Hex #8E8E93

  • Gray 700 (Icons): Hex #3A3A3C

  • Accent Colors (For Tags/Folders):

  • Use the native iOS system colors (Indigo, Teal, Orange, Purple) to maintain familiar affordances.

Typography

  • Primary Typeface: Inter (or Apple's SF Pro natively in-app). Used for all product UI and functional marketing. Clean, highly legible, modern geometric sans-serif.
  • Secondary Typeface: Newsreader (Display/Editorial). A crisp serif used sparingly for large overarching brand statements or report covers to ground the digital product in the physical reality of real estate contracts.
  • Usage Hierarchy:
  • Display: Newsreader Medium, tightly tracked.
  • Headline 1: Inter Bold.
  • Body text: Inter Regular, 150% line height.
  • Captions/Labels: Inter Medium, uppercase, widely tracked.

Imagery Style

  • Photography: "In context, in motion." Bright, naturally lit environments showing real estate professionals in action (at a property finish line, in a car looking at an iPad). The tone is focused, not staged corporate smiling. Depth of field should blur messy backgrounds to emphasize the device/document.
  • Illustration Style: isometric, architectural line-drawings using Blueprint Blue and System Mint. Extremely precise and structured.
  • Iconography: SF Symbols-based geometric outlines. 1.5pt line weight, 2px corner radius. Filled versions only used for active states.
  • Patterns: A subtle, 5% opacity "grid" or "blueprint mesh" used in large empty spaces or presentation backgrounds to reinforce structure.

3. BRAND APPLICATIONS

  • Business Cards:
  • Front: White background, Charcoal Logo centered, subtle grid pattern.
  • Back: Real Estate Navy background, Contact details in white, Inter font, minimal spacing.
  • Letterhead & Stationery:
  • Crisp white paper. Charcoal logo top left, left-aligned contact info in Gray 500 bottom left. 1-inch margins minimum.
  • Email Signature:
  • Full Name (Inter Bold, Charcoal)
  • Title | Bricks Scan (Inter Regular, Gray 500)
  • "Structure the chaos." (System Mint)
  • Social Media Templates:
  • Avatar: White 'B' monogram on System Mint background.
  • Cover Photo (LinkedIn/Twitter): Isometric blueprint illustration of a building seamlessly transitioning into a digital document.
  • Presentation Template:
  • Title Slide: Navy background, large Newsreader serif title, grid pattern overlay.
  • Content Slide: Paper white, tight Charcoal headers, generous whitespace.
  • Data/Numbers Slide: System Mint accents highlighting key metrics, bold Inter typography for numbers.
  • Closing Slide: Monochromatic Charcoal logo, simple call to action "Get Bricks Scan on the App Store."
