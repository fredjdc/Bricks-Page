# Bricks Apps: Social Media Playbook

**Objective:** Consistently publish 2 posts per week across LinkedIn, X, and Instagram without violating the [Brand Voice Guide](brand-voice-guide.md). 

Remember: We do not sell. We state facts. We earn trust through restraint.

---

## 1. The 2-Post Weekly Cadence

To avoid sounding repetitive while rotating through three separate apps, stick to a predictable alternating schedule. Do not pitch the "suite"—focus on one app per post.

### Post 1 (e.g., Tuesday): The Feature Focus
Highlight exactly one feature of one app.
*   **Calc:** "Compare two loan scenarios side-by-side."
*   **Scan:** "Crop and straighten documents automatically."
*   **Leads:** "Filter contacts by 'needs follow-up'."

### Post 2 (e.g., Thursday): The Anti-Status Quo (Our Philosophy)
Highlight *how* the app works, contrasting it with industry norms (without being aggressively negative).
*   **Calc:** "No account required. Calculate numbers instantly."
*   **Scan:** "Your documents stay on your device. Local-first."
*   **Leads:** "No subscriptions. Runs offline."

---

## 2. The Copywriting Formula

When drafting your 2 posts per week, use this strict structural formula. If a sentence doesn't fit one of these three steps, delete it.

1.  **The Action:** What does the app do? (e.g., *Scan, organize, and export real estate documents.*)
2.  **The Constraint (The 'Why'):** Why is our way better? (e.g., *Your files stay on your device. No cloud dependency.*)
3.  **The CTA:** (e.g., *Download Bricks Scan on the App Store.*)

**Self-Correction Checklist:**
*   [ ] Did I use the word "seamless," "ultimate," or "powerful"? (If yes, delete).
*   [ ] Is the post about more than one app? (If yes, split it into two posts).
*   [ ] Does the image match the text exactly?

## 3. Four-Week Topic Rotation

If you don't know what to post, follow this 4-week loop to ensure each app gets coverage:

### Week 1: Bricks Calc
*   **Post 1 (Feature):** Amortization schedules. (Show a screenshot of the schedule).
    *   *EN:* "View complete amortization schedules. Show clients exact principal and interest breakdowns. Download Bricks Calc on the App Store."
    *   *ES:* "Revisa cronogramas de amortización completos. Muestra a tus clientes el desglose exacto de capital e intereses. Descarga Bricks Calc."
*   **Post 2 (Anti-Status Quo):** Speed / No login.
    *   *EN:* "Calculate monthly payments instantly. No accounts, no waiting. Download Bricks Calc on the App Store."
    *   *ES:* "Calcula cuotas mensuales al instante. Sin cuentas, sin esperas. Descarga Bricks Calc."

### Week 2: Bricks Scan
*   **Post 1 (Feature):** Multi-page exports. (Show a video of exporting a PDF).
    *   *EN:* "Scan multiple pages and export as a single PDF. Download Bricks Scan on the App Store."
    *   *ES:* "Escanea múltiples páginas y exporta en un solo PDF. Descarga Bricks Scan."
*   **Post 2 (Anti-Status Quo):** Privacy / Local-first.
    *   *EN:* "Your contracts belong on your phone, not in a cloud database. Bricks Scan runs locally. Download on the App Store."
    *   *ES:* "Tus contratos pertenecen en tu teléfono, no en una base de datos en la nube. Bricks Scan funciona localmente. Descarga en el App Store."

### Week 3: Bricks Leads (Beta)
*   **Post 1 (Feature):** Simple pipeline. (Show the pipeline view).
    *   *EN:* "Track every lead from first contact to close. Built for realtors. Try the Bricks Leads Beta on the App Store."
    *   *ES:* "Haz seguimiento a cada contacto desde el inicio hasta el cierre. Creado para agentes inmobiliarios. Prueba la versión Beta."
*   **Post 2 (Anti-Status Quo):** No subscription.
    *   *EN:* "A CRM that doesn't charge you a monthly fee to view your own contacts. Runs locally. Try the Bricks Leads Beta."
    *   *ES:* "Un CRM que no te cobra una tarifa mensual por ver tus propios contactos. Funciona localmente. Prueba la versión Beta."

### Week 4: Use Cases / Scenarios
*   **Post 1 (Calc):** 
    *   *EN:* "Client asks for real numbers at a showing. Calculate payments and compare loans on the spot. Works offline."
    *   *ES:* "El cliente pide números reales durante la visita. Calcula pagos y compara préstamos al instante. Funciona sin conexión."
*   **Post 2 (Scan):** 
    *   *EN:* "Need to send a signed disclosure from the car? Scan, crop, and email in one tap."
    *   *ES:* "¿Necesitas enviar una divulgación firmada desde el auto? Escanea, recorta y envía por correo con un toque."

## 4. Visual Assets Rulebook

Because the copy is minimal, the visuals must carry the weight.
1.  **No Stock Photos:** Do not use photos of people shaking hands, generic buildings, or holding keys. 
2.  **Use Real Screenshots:** Show the actual UI. If the copy says "compare loans," the image must show the loan comparison screen.
3.  **Keep it Clean:** Use a simple, solid-color background (using the app's specific accent color) behind a phone mockup if needed.
