# BRAND VOICE GUIDE — Bricks Apps

Last updated: April 2026

The shared rulebook that makes every piece of Bricks Apps copy sound like it came from the same person. The agent reads this before writing anything. It does not change per app. It applies equally to Bricks Calc, Bricks Scan, and Bricks Leads.

It is not a style guide. It is not a tone wheel. It is a set of clear, enforceable rules that any agent or writer can apply without interpretation.

---

## WHO WE ARE

Bricks Apps builds focused, native iOS tools for real estate professionals. Every app does one thing well. No bloat, no subscriptions, no accounts. Local-first by default, professional by design.

---

## WHO WE WRITE FOR

Realtors, mortgage brokers, and real estate professionals who work on their feet. They are busy, skeptical of software promises, and have been burned by apps that require sign-ins, charge monthly, or stop working offline. They value speed, reliability, and tools that respect their time. They do not read marketing copy carefully. They scan it for proof that the app does what it says.

---

## VOICE IN ONE SENTENCE

Clear, calm, and professional, built for people who work, not people who browse.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES

1. State what it does. Then why it matters. Then stop.
   Do not add a third reason when two are sufficient. Do not add enthusiasm where none is earned.

2. Plain words over impressive words.
   If a simpler word exists, use it. Clarity is the product. Obscurity is a defect.

3. Specificity over generality.
   "Scan multi-page documents in one session" is stronger than "powerful scanning." Specific claims are credible. General claims are ignored.

4. Respect the user's intelligence.
   Do not explain what a mortgage is. Do not over-qualify. Write for someone who already knows their job.

5. Earn trust through restraint.
   Hype signals insecurity. Calm confidence signals quality. The fewer adjectives, the more believable the copy.

6. Local-first is a feature, not a footnote.
   No account. No subscription. No cloud dependency. This is a real differentiator. Say it plainly and early.

## DO / DO NOT

| DO | DO NOT |
|---|---|
| Scan. Organize. Share. | The ultimate scanning experience. |
| No account required. | Hassle-free setup. |
| Built for realtors. | Designed for everyone. |
| Works offline. | Available anywhere, anytime. |
| Calculate monthly payments. | Powerful mortgage tools at your fingertips. |
| Your data stays on device. | Privacy you can trust. |
| See your pipeline. | Get a bird's-eye view of your workflow. |
| One tap to share. | Sharing has never been easier. |

---

## WORD LIST — USE

scan, calculate, organize, track, share, export, view, manage, capture, store, search, filter, remind, close, local, offline, native, fast, focused, professional, clean, reliable, simple, direct, precise

---

## WORD LIST — AVOID

powerful, seamless, intuitive, effortless, robust, ultimate, best-in-class, supercharge, streamline, revolutionize, game-changing, cutting-edge, smart, beautiful, delightful, amazing, easy, hassle-free, anywhere anytime, all-in-one, next-level, reimagined

Experience the most powerful mortgage calculator designed to supercharge your real estate workflow.

## HOW THE AGENT USES THIS DOCUMENT

The agent reads the Brand Voice Guide before it reads anything else. It applies the Do / Do Not pairs as a filter on every sentence it writes. It checks every output against Word List — Avoid before finalizing. If a sentence feels weak, it runs it against the Example Rewrites for calibration.

This document requires no interpretation. Every rule is a constraint, not a suggestion.
